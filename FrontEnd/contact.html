Contact and further information

Katharsis is a tool for the quantitative analysis of historic German plays with a sentiment analysis compenent specifically for Lessing's plays. Katharsis is a pre-release version. The tool is currently under development and is constantly changing functionality and design.

The following persons participated in the creation of Katharsis:
  • Thomas Schmidt (M. Sc.) of the University of Regensburg
  • Jun.-Prof. Dr. Manuel Burghardt of the University of Leipzig
  • PD Dr. Katrin Dennerlein of the University of Würzburg
  • Prof. Dr. Christian Wolff of the University of Regensburg

If you want to use the tool or results of the tool in any form in research, teaching or for other purposes please contact us before doing so. Please cite one of the papers below when using the tool for research purposes.

You can also contact us if you have any questions or problems:
Mail (Thomas Schmidt): thomas.schmidt@ur.de
